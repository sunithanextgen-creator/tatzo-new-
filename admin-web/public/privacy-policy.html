Privacy Policy

Tatzo is operated by SUNITHA PACKIAM. This policy explains how Tatzo collects, uses, and protects user data.

Data we collect

We may collect account details such as name, email address, phone number, profile details, city/area location, artist profile information, posts/images, booking information, verification request details, likes, follows, notifications, and payment status or transaction references.

How we use data

We use data to create and secure accounts, help users discover tattoo artists, manage booking requests, support artist verification, show posts and notifications, process payment status, provide support, prevent abuse, and improve Tatzo.

Payments

Payments may be processed using Razorpay or other trusted payment providers. Tatzo does not store full card, UPI, or banking credentials. Payment providers may process payment data according to their own policies.

Health and skin-check information

Any skin-check or tattoo safety information in Tatzo is for informational and booking-support purposes only. It is not medical advice and does not replace consultation with a qualified healthcare professional.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with service providers such as Firebase, hosting, analytics, storage, authentication, and payment providers when necessary to operate the app, comply with law, protect users, or prevent fraud.

Security

We use encryption in transit and access controls where available through our service providers. Users should keep their login details secure and contact us if they suspect unauthorised access.

Data deletion

Users can request account and data deletion at Tatzo Account & Data Deletion or by emailing sunithanextgen@gmail.com.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact sunithanextgen@gmail.com or visit Tatzo Support.
