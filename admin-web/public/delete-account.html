Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how users can request deletion of their Tatzo account and associated data.

App name: Tatzo

Developer: SUNITHA PACKIAM

Contact email: sunithanextgen@gmail.com

Support page: Tatzo Support

How to request account deletion

  1. Email us at sunithanextgen@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete my Tatzo account.
  3. Include the email address or phone number used for your Tatzo account so we can identify it.

What data will be deleted

When your request is verified, we will delete or anonymise account data associated with your Tatzo account where legally possible, including profile information, saved location details, posts, likes, follows, notifications, booking profile data, and verification request information.

Data that may be retained

Some records may be retained for a limited period when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, tax, payment dispute handling, or audit purposes. Payment records and transaction references may be retained for up to 90 days or longer if required by applicable law or payment provider obligations.

Delete specific data without deleting your account

You may also request deletion of specific data, such as posts, profile details, or verification request data, without deleting your full account. Email us with the subject line Delete specific Tatzo data and describe what you want removed.
